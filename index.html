Welcome to Stratego: Lite
A Strategy-Based Game

The objective is to collect the opponents flag!

Rules:
- Each player will have a 1 Flag, 1 Bomb, 1 Captan, and 7 Major pieces.
Bombs and Flags are fixed and cannot be moved once placed.
- Each player can move one piece per turn, 1 space in any direction, as long as the space is not occupied.
- The 4 Water squares can never be occupied.
- If a piece is occupied by another players piece, you may click on it to 'challenge' it.
- Captains have the highest value, followed by Majors.
- Captains will beat Majors.
- If both pieces are the same, both will die.
- Bombs will kill any piece that come into contact with it.
- If you capture the other players flag, you win!
- If, in a rare turn of events, both players have no pieces left - the game will end in a draw
- When ready to test your skills, press start to initiate the game.
